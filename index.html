Invitation Card

November 18, 2024

This activity focuses on creating an eye-catching and informative invitation for an upcoming event. Using Inkscape, the design should not only detail the event clearly but also engage the audience through creative use of colors, typography, and layout principles. The goal is to merge aesthetic appeal with effective communication.

Philippine Flag

November 18, 2024

Design a scalable digital representation of the Philippine flag using Inkscape. This task requires creating a precise and detailed rendition of the flag's key elements, including its blue and red horizontal bands, white equilateral triangle, golden sun with eight rays, and three five-pointed stars. The objective is to accurately depict these elements while maintaining the correct proportions and colors to honor the flag's symbolic significance.

Provide Orthographic Drawing

November 12, 2024

In this activity, we have been given the challenge of visualizing a 3D object and representing it effectively in a 2D format. This involves creating accurate illustrations that showcase the object from three critical perspectives: the front view, side view, and top view.
